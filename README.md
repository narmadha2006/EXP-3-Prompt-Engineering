# EXP-3-PROMPT-ENGINEERING-

## Aim: 
Evaluation of 2024 Prompting Tools Across Diverse AI Platforms: 
ChatGPT, Claude, Bard, Cohere Command, and Meta
Experiment:
Within a specific use case (e.g., summarizing text, answering technical questions), compare the performance, user experience, and response quality of prompting tools across these different AI platforms.

## Algorithm:

1) Select Platforms & Use Cases — Choose the AI platforms to compare (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cohere, Meta) and define the specific use cases to test, such as summarization, technical Q&A, creative writing, and customer support.

2) Design Two Prompt Versions — For each use case, create two prompts: a broad/vague prompt with minimal instruction, and a refined/structured prompt with clear role, format, constraints, and audience defined.

3) Run & Collect Responses — Feed both prompt versions into each platform under the same conditions and collect all responses, noting instruction adherence, word count, tone, and factual accuracy.

4) Score Each Response — Evaluate every response across four dimensions — Quality, Accuracy, Depth, and Consistency — assigning a percentage score to each, then calculate the average per platform per use case.

5) Compare & Conclude — Aggregate all scores into a master table, identify which platform performs best per use case, measure the quality lift between broad and refined prompts, and recommend the right platform for each real-world scenario.

## Prompt
**1.ChatGpt:**

You are an expert in education technology.

Task: Explain the impact of Artificial Intelligence on modern education.

Provide a well-structured answer with:
1. Introduction
2. Positive Impacts
3. Challenges
4. Real-world Examples
5. Conclusion

Ensure clarity, logical flow, and detailed explanation.

**2.Claude:**

You are a research assistant.

Context:
Artificial Intelligence is transforming the education sector globally.

Task:
Explain its impact on modern education.

Include:
- Overview
- Benefits
- Limitations or challenges
- Examples
- Summary

Make the explanation clear, thoughtful, and easy to understand.

**3.Gemini(Bard):**

You are a research assistant.

Context:
Artificial Intelligence is transforming the education sector globally.

Task:
Explain its impact on modern education.

Include:
- Overview
- Benefits
- Limitations or challenges
- Examples
- Summary

Make the explanation clear, thoughtful, and easy to understand.

**4.Meta:**

Explain how Artificial Intelligence affects education.

Include:
- Benefits
- Challenges
- Examples
- Conclusion

Keep it concise and structured.

## Result
The experiment showed that different AI platforms produce varied outputs for the same topic, with differences in structure, clarity, and depth of explanation. Optimized prompts improved response quality, highlighting that each AI performs best with prompt styles tailored to its strengths.
